About NGO Connect

Empowering communities, transforming lives

Our Story

Founded in 2010, NGO Care4All has been at the forefront of community development, working tirelessly to create positive change in the lives of thousands. What started as a small initiative has grown into a movement that touches lives across multiple communities.

Our journey has been marked by challenges and triumphs, but through it all, our commitment to making a difference has remained unwavering.

Our Impact

Education

  • Established 50+ learning centers across 5 states
  • Benefiting 5,000+ children annually with quality education
  • 95% of students show improved academic performance
  • Provided 1,000+ scholarships to underprivileged students

Healthcare

  • Provided medical care to over 10,000 individuals in rural areas
  • Conducted 50+ health camps in underserved communities
  • Distributed essential medicines to 2,500+ families
  • Trained 200+ community health workers

Environment

  • Planted 10,000+ trees across deforested areas
  • Conducted environmental awareness programs in 20+ schools
  • Established 5 community gardens in urban areas
  • Reduced plastic waste by 60% in target communities
